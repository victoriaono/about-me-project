Short Term Goals

I want to explore the city because I live in one of the greatest cities in the world, and there are so much that I have not yet seen. I want to visit more neighborhoods in Brooklyn such as Williamsburg. I want to explore more of the villages in Manhattan (East Village, Greenwich Village) and try all the unique desserts available there. I also want to go to Top of the Rock and see the New York skyscrapers.
I love taking photos with my Nikon camera, and I enjoy taking photos whenever I go to new places. When the weather is nice, I want to go to all of these places in the city and take photos.

I have a lot of books on my bookshelf that I own but have not read, and there is dust accumulating on them. Sadly, because of school, I am usually not able to read. I want to spend more time alone and escape to a world of great stories. I would like to read all of my unread books (about 15) by the end of the summer.

Ever since listening to the cast album of Hamilton and crying my eyes out last year, I have been obsessed. It is extremely hard to get tickets, and even though there are some resale tickets available towards the end of the year, they are very expensive. I only rely on the lottery, hoping that at some point I will win and see this phenonemal show.

Long Term Goals

I have come to realize that I cannot live outside of a big city. I've visited a few college campuses and found them wonderful, but I know that I would not survive a year in those places. So, I want to stay in New York and go to a college in the metro area so I would not have to leave home. I am also hoping to have enough money to rent a nice apartment of my own, possibly to share with a friend. Either way, when I grow up, I want to get a large apartment with great views, and put a grand piano in the living room. I also want another puppy so I could go on walks in Central Park. I like chihuahuas because they are small like me.

In college, when I have longer breaks, I want to visit as many places in the world as I can. I have never been to Europe, so I want to tour most, if not all, of Europe, just like my mom did when she was in college. I want to use my knowledge of the French language to talk to people in France, and expand my knowledge of European history. I also want to tour Asia because I went to China in 2010 and found that it was quite different from Japan. I want to observe the wild nature and try their food.
